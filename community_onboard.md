## What This Community Is

The tokenX community exists to:

- explore new ways of coordinating participation
- support builders, creators, and organizers
- experiment openly, without guarantees
- treat culture as infrastructure
- allow the free market to exist without letting it dominate everything

This is not a fan club.  
It is not a pump group.  
It is not a DAO (yet, maybe never).

It is a place to **do things**.

## What Participation Means

Participation does **not** mean buying a token.

Participation can mean:

- reading and understanding the charter
- asking thoughtful questions
- building something small
- hosting a meetup or discussion
- creating art, writing, or remixing ideas
- critiquing tokenX honestly and publicly
- experimenting responsibly

Holding tokenX does not grant status.
Contribution does.
