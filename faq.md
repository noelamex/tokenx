# tokenX — FAQ

## 1. What is tokenX?

tokenX is a public crypto token and network designed to enable participation, coordination, and cultural expression across the Caribbean and its global diaspora.

It is a shared primitive people can use to build, experiment, and coordinate — openly and without permission.

---

## 2. Is tokenX an investment?

No.

tokenX does not promise returns, yield, dividends, or profit sharing.  
Its value, if any, is determined by how people choose to use it.

Participation involves risk.

---

## 3. Why does tokenX exist?

tokenX exists to explore a simple question:

> What happens if people have a shared way to coordinate culture, projects, and participation — without relying on traditional gatekeepers?

It is an experiment, not a finished solution.

---

## 4. Is tokenX a currency?

No.

tokenX is not meant to replace local currencies or be used for everyday purchases like groceries.  
It functions as a coordination and participation token, not legal tender.

---

## 5. Who can participate?

Anyone.

tokenX is open to:
- individuals
- builders
- creators
- communities
- retail and institutional participants

No approval is required.

---

## 6. What can I actually do with tokenX?

Depending on how the ecosystem evolves, tokenX can be used to:
- stake to signal participation
- register projects or events
- access communities or experiences
- participate in cultural or experimental initiatives

tokenX is designed to be used, not just held.

---

## 7. Is there a fixed supply?

Yes.

The total supply of tokenX is fixed at **10,000,000,000 tokens**.  
There is no inflation.

Allocations and vesting schedules are transparent and on-chain.

---

## 8. Who controls tokenX?

No single person controls tokenX.

The network is stewarded by the **tokenX Foundation**, which supports ecosystem development and infrastructure. The Foundation does not control token price or promise outcomes.

The direction of tokenX depends on its participants.

---

## 9. What blockchain is tokenX on?

tokenX is initially deployed on the **Sui blockchain**, chosen for performance and developer experience.

The design is blockchain-agnostic, and tokenX may expand to other chains in the future.

---

## 10. What are the risks?

Like all crypto assets, tokenX involves risks, including:
- market volatility
- technical risk
- regulatory uncertainty
- social and cultural dynamics

Participation is voluntary.  
No guarantees are made.

---

*tokenX does not promise returns. Participation is at your own risk.*
