Why tokenX exists

tokenX started as a question: what happens if people have a shared primitive to coordinate culture, commerce, and community across the Caribbean and its diaspora?

Coordination, not extraction

The Caribbean is rich in culture and activity, but much value leaks outward. tokenX experiments with a shared coordination layer — open, permissionless, and built for participation.

Culture as infrastructure

Music, festivals, art, stories — these are not decorations. They are how people coordinate. tokenX treats culture as a first-class part of network growth.
