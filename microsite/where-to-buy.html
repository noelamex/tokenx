Primary Ways to Acquire tokenX

1. Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)

tokenX is initially deployed on the Sui blockchain. The most direct way to acquire tokenX is through decentralized exchanges (DEXs) on Sui.

  • You connect a self-custody wallet
  • You swap SUI or another supported asset for tokenX
  • You retain full control of your tokens

Supported DEXs will be listed here once liquidity is live.

2. Centralized Exchanges (CEX)

tokenX may be listed on centralized exchanges in the future. Listings depend on exchange requirements and timelines and are never guaranteed.

  • Account creation and identity verification (KYC) are required
  • You deposit funds (fiat or crypto)
  • You trade for tokenX through the exchange interface

tokenX will only be listed on exchanges that meet transparency and compliance standards.
