# Where to Buy tokenX

tokenX is a public participation token.  
Acquiring tokenX is optional and involves risk.

---

## Important Reminder

tokenX does not promise:
- returns
- yield
- dividends
- price appreciation

Only acquire tokenX if you understand the risks.

---

## Primary Ways to Acquire tokenX

### 1. Decentralized Exchanges (DEX)

tokenX is initially deployed on the **Sui blockchain**.

The most direct way to acquire tokenX is through decentralized exchanges on Sui:

- connect a self-custody wallet
- swap SUI or supported assets for tokenX
- retain full control of your tokens

**DEX listings will be published here once live.**

---

### 2. Centralized Exchanges (CEX)

tokenX may be listed on centralized exchanges in the future.

- listings are not guaranteed
- exchanges require KYC/AML
- timelines depend on exchange policies

No exchange listings exist at launch.

---

## Wallets

To hold tokenX, you need a Sui-compatible wallet:

- Sui Wallet
- Martian Wallet
- Ethos Wallet

Always download wallets from official sources.

---

## Verify Before You Buy

- Only use the official tokenX contract address
- Never trust unsolicited messages
- Watch for fake tokens with similar names

The official contract address will be published on the tokenX site.

---

## Step-by-Step (High Level)

1. Install a supported wallet  
2. Acquire SUI for transaction fees  
3. Connect your wallet to a supported DEX  
4. Swap for tokenX  
5. Confirm the transaction
